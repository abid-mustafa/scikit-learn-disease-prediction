Our project endeavors to develop a disease diagnosis system utilizing machine learning algorithms to predict disease likelihood based on input symptoms. The primary goal is to provide users with valuable insights into their health conditions, enabling them to seek timely medical assistance.

Currently, there are similar systems available, such as Disease Prediction.ai and Symptoms Checker. While these systems offer valuable services, they may suffer from limitations such as restricted disease coverage and variable data quality.

Existing systems may lack comprehensive disease coverage and face challenges related to data quality and representativeness. These limitations underscore the need for a more robust and inclusive solution.

Our proposed solution aims to overcome existing limitations by expanding disease coverage and implementing rigorous data validation techniques. By incorporating new features and enhancements, we strive to improve prediction accuracy and reliability.

Salient Features:

  • User-friendly Interface: Our system provides an intuitive interface for inputting symptoms and receiving disease predictions.
  • Comprehensive Disease Coverage: We cover a wide range of medical conditions to cater to diverse user populations effectively.
  • Recommended Treatments: Based on chosen datasets, we offer recommended treatments for identified diseases.
  • Continuous Improvement: Our system undergoes continuous refinement through data validation and model refinement processes.
  • Integration with Healthcare Professionals: Seamless integration with healthcare professionals allows for further consultation or guidance.

Tools & Technologies:

  • Programming Language: Python
  • Frameworks: Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Flask (for web application development)
  • Operating System: Windows
